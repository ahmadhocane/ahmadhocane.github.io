Research Experience

I am currently working as a Research Fellow at Brigham and Women’s Hospital and Harvard Medical School, where I contribute to clinical trials and translational research in interventional radiology, particularly in embolization therapies.

My prior research experience includes work as a research assistant at LUMS, where I studied immune impairments in type 2 diabetes and their role in infectious disease susceptibility.

I have also gained laboratory experience in histopathology, tissue engineering, genetics, and molecular biology techniques including PCR, ELISA, and flow cytometry.
